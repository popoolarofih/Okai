Our Services

Printer

Elevate your printing game with our selection of top-notch DTF printers. Ink

Discover a world of vibrant colors and exceptional quality with our DTF ink selection. Choose from water-based, eco-solvent, and even fluorescent inks to create eye-catching designs. Our inks are formulated for optimal adhesion and washfastness on various fabrics, ensuring your prints stay bright and bold.

Film Roll

Find the perfect base for your DTF transfers with our selection of high-quality film rolls. Available in various sizes to match your printer and project requirements. Our film offers excellent printability and transferability, ensuring a smooth and seamless workflow from design to finished product.

Maintainance and Installation

Maximize your printer's lifespan and ensure optimal performance with our professional maintenance and installation services. Our experienced technicians can handle everything from routine maintenance to setting up your new DTF printer for efficient operation. We'll ensure your printer is running flawlessly so you can focus on creating amazing prints.
